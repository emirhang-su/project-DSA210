Here are the public mods (addons) selected for analysis. For enrichment and aggregation purposes we will include a certain number of addons for certain number of addon types. The addon types include:

-Gamemode
-Map
-Weapon
-Vehicle
-NPC
-Tool
-Entity
-Effects
-Model
-Server content

For each these 10 addon types we will choose 5 animated and 5 still image thumbnails, so we will end up with 100 addons in total. There are also some criteria we think should be limited by for choosing:

1. The addon must not be an obvious requirement(dependency) for other addons. These include things like weapon/NPC/vehicle bases and GLua libraries. Since we are primarily studying the effects of thumbnails, choosing extremely popular required addons will steer us away from our goal.

2. The addon must be released in calendar year 2025. This is to provide some scope for the study.

3. The addon must have a star rating of over 3 stars. Check PersonalModList.txt for more information regarding stars.
