- 3 Wagons (1 taxi)
- 2 Hatchbacks
- 1 Coupe
- 6 SUVs (1 taxi, 2 police version, 1 pickup)
- 2 Pickups
- 1 Limousine
- 2 Vans (2 police, 1 news and others)

- Many trailers for semi-trailers
- Several versions of ambulance
- 2 Buses (1 police, 1 school)

- 1 Military APC with a working machine gun

- 2 Military trucks with several versions
