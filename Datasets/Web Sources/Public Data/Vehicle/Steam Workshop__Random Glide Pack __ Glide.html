Description
Big pack of commissioned cars.

The vehicles are in the Exidnost category.

Engine sounds and other sounds were taken from Randy's L4D2

New Update Vehicles:

- 1970 GMC Jimmy by Artemoz5
- 1979 Chevrolet Camaro Z28
- 1982 Ford Bronco XLT
- 1985 Chevrolet El Camino
- 1986 Buick Century Limited by carface80[www.gta5-mods.com]
- 1993 BMW M3 Coupe by Ddiaz Design[sketchfab.com]
- Ford LTD Crown Victoria ( Civil, Police )
- Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution X (FQ400) by temich[sketchfab.com]
- Plymouth Roadrunner 383
- Volvo 850

Pre-update Vehicles:

- 1967 Shelby GT500 by OUTPISTON[sketchfab.com]
- 1989 Mazda MX5 by OUTPISTON[sketchfab.com]
- 2006 Chevrolet Impala LS by OUTPISTON[sketchfab.com]
- 2024 Ducati StreetFighter V4 S by OUTPISTON[sketchfab.com]
- Jeep Cherokee XJ + Black Mesa version by codexito[sketchfab.com]
- 1998 Caprice Station Wagon by Doz3[sketchfab.com]
- 1993 Ford Mustang GT (Foxbody) by Randomness[sketchfab.com]
- Chevrolet Corvette C4 by Randomness[sketchfab.com]
- 1990 Chevrolet S-10 by n1ck[sketchfab.com]
- Chapman '73 + Police version by Daniel Zhabotinsky[sketchfab.com]
- 2002 Honda Odyssey by jrem7315[www.gta5-mods.com]
- 1999 Opel Omega B + Sport version by HUMSTER3D
- 1998 Opel Astra G by HUMSTER3D

Features:
- Animated pop-up headlights on the Mazda MX5 and Chevrolet Corvette
- Some cars have bones that allow you to open doors, hoods and trunks. Use the Advanced Bonemerge Tool or Advanced Bone Tool
- Custom engine sounds
- Glowing textures for headlights
- Steering wheels are animated
- Some cars have an animated speedometer and tachometer

Maps on background:
- Paralake City V6 Night
- Friluft
- RP_NYCity
- Black Mesa - Questionable Ethics

I don't take suggestion but
If you'd like me to do something, I can make a commission addon. More details are in my Boosty[boosty.to].
