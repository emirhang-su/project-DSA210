Description
About Addon:
If you're tired of being on the rebel side and you want to play for the other side? The addon is at your service, this addon allows you to change your faction. But you should understand that it is better not to install addons that change the AI relationships and behavior of NPCs, this may break the addon.

The Factions You Can Currently Play On:
The Combine,
Resistance,
Outcast,
Neutral,
Zombies/Headcrabs,
Antlions,
H.E.C.U, (You Need Have Half-Life Source Or Half-Life Resurgence)
Xen, (You Need Have Half-Life Source Or Half-Life Resurgence)
Race X, (You Need Have Half-Life Resurgence)
Black Operations, (You Need Have Half-Life Resurgence)
Aperture Science. (You Need Have Portal)

Features:
Support for all NPCs, SNPCs and there is support for ZBase and VJ Base, I don't know about ANP, but it doesn't seem to work.
You can change the faction via the spawn menu or via the pbcr_setfaction command.
The name of the npc is not touched. I made it work through CLASS_* and through the Classify function.
There is a separate tab in the spawn menu where you can change the faction.

Frequent Questions:
> Does it work in multiplayer?
AHTOXA: Yes, the addon works in multiplayer mode.
> Is there localization into other languages?
Aiden Walker 3650: Yes, there are examples in Russian, Ukrainian, German, and English, and there will be other languages in the future.
> What should I do if this addon does not work?
Aiden Walker 3650: If your addon isn't working... It means you have addons that change NPC relationships and AI behavior. These addons can break my addon, but if you have two addons that allow you to change player factions, unfortunately they may conflict. You will have to choose one addon to avoid conflicts.
> Will there be other factions?
Aiden Walker 3650: To be honest, there are some that fit and are suitable, but the factions are not endless. I have already added most of them, only a small part remains.
> Will there be updates and improvements to this addon?
Aiden Walker 3650: Yes, there will be updates, improvements, and fixes for bugs and shortcomings.
> Are all NPCs and SNPCs working properly?
Aiden Walker 3650: Yes, all NPCs and SNPCs work, but there is one tiny nuance: if the NPC is custom and changes its relationships through the NPC name and the player, then most likely the NPC will not work, but otherwise most NPCs and SNPCs work normally, unless, of course, you have installed addons that change the AI behavior of NPCs and NPCs relationships.
> Will NextBots be supported?
Aiden Walker 3650: No, no, and no. Because I don't know how to implement it, and if I did, it would probably break everything, so I'm not going to add support for it, sorry.

Planned In This Addon:
The Betrayal System.
Replacing The Current Saving Of Factions With Saving Factions On PlayerModels.
Adding All Localization Languages.
Adding ANP support (Add NPC Plus) If Possible.
