Description
Ever wished you could go back in time? Visit the Everpine Mall today

A huge fully explorable mall , no inaccesible areas besides the skybox :)
With fully detailed shop interiors, with over 300 custom models.
Bring your friends! And have fun!

All custom assets were made by me
With additional assets taken from CS:GO , CS:S , L4D2 and HL:2 ep2

Music included as part of the map ambience:
When by Earl Sheldon
Café Bossa by Riverworn
Ipanema Daydream by Bird Creek
The One You Say Good Morning To by Muzak Orchestra
Welcome Back by Muzak Orchestra
They should all be relatively copyright-free

Quick QnA

Why's the map over 800mb?
(Remember those times? Its now 400mb lol )

Over 300 custom made models, custom textures, and assets taken from source games, in addition to the navmesh, ai nodes, and lighting information. Yeah it adds up really quickly.

Ai nodes? Nav mesh?
Yes

Game content required?
Everything should be packed into the map, but if you wanna be safe : CS:GO,CS:S,L4D2,EP2

Other game mode versions?
I don't plan on making any, but if i see high enough demands i might
And no, you may not port it yourself

Back to the future reference?
Not really, tha name and logo correlation was accidental lol

Toggleable Lights?
My intention wasn't to make a dark and spooky mall, but a comfy and chill one
Additionaly it could cause severe lighting issue, and bloat the file size even more

Secrets?
Outside of a few developer screenshots you can find, no, not this time lol

May i use the assets in my own map?
No i would rather not, but contact me directly or through comments if you wanna inquire further.

Can i Reupload / Edit / Port the map?
No, absolutley not!
I will be contacting moderation if this happens, ive done it before.

If you decide to make content using the map, let me know! (Youtube Videos or Art) I would love to see it
