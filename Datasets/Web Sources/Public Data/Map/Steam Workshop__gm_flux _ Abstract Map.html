About This Map
I am a huge fan of abstract maps in the Source Engine. So I've created one myself! I took some inspiration from a whole bunch of classic abstract maps by Nipper and Shadgrimgrvy and many others.

Features
  • 50+ unique rooms
  • Cool map
  • Custom music (disable with music volume in game settings)
  • Custom soundscapes
  • NPC Nodes
  • NAV Mesh

Credits
Map: Me
Textures: Valve, TopHattWaffle, Me
Models: Valve

Music Credits:
  • Jazzpunk
  • Boneworks
  • Tower Unite
  • Super Plumber Man 64
  • Red Dead Redemption 2
  • Deltarune
  • Minecraft
  • LSD Dream Emulator
  • PS1
  • And a few more that I cannot mention here

Extras
Everything is packed into this map, so you do not need to mount any games.

I may have forgotten who made some assets in the map due to the garrysmods.org website shutting down. If anyone knows who these original authors are, please let me know and I will update the description!

Check out the mixtape for this map here: https://youtu.be/wybL48DKCcU

Check out The Librarian's exploration of the map here: https://youtu.be/F4L1w30WEcw

Do NOT reupload this map ANYWHERE without permission please.
