Description
A short, story-driven map with multiple endings.

If you enjoy the map, don't forget to like and favorite it! That is the best support for me.

  • The map is designed for a single player. Playing in multiplayer may break the scripts.
  • It is highly recommended to disable any other addons.
  • Estimated playtime: ~15 minutes.

--- PLOT ---
You are an ordinary soldier stationed at a nuclear testing ground near the city of Zhdanek.
Your duty is to remain vigilant and, in the event of a missile threat, launch a retaliatory strike against the enemy. It's usually a cushy job, and you spend most of your shift watching various shows on the TV. But for the past few weeks, everyone has been on edge—a neighboring country is amassing troops at the border. According to their statements, it's for military exercises; according to intelligence reports, it's for an offensive operation.

--- OTHER ---
Write "conflict_restrictions 0" in the console to enable the ability to no-clip and create objects.
Also, the command "ent_fire disable_story_end setvalue 1" disables the ending.
