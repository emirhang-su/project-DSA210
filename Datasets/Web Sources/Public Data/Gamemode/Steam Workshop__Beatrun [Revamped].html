Description
Beatrun: Revamped

Beatrun is a reworked and enhanced version of the classic parkour mod for Garry’s Mod.
Wall-running, vaults, slides, and tricks are now smoother, faster, and more dynamic than ever before.

Join our Discord Community!

renovation

Feature's

Fully reworked parkour system: wall-runs, vaults, wall-kicks, and slides.

Improved animations for more cinematic movement.

Enhanced jump physics and momentum system.

Works on a variety of maps.

Compatible with other addons

Who is it for ?

Fans of Mirror’s Edge and fast-paced movement.

Players who want to turn Garry’s Mod into a true parkour playground.

Servers looking for competitive or fun freerunning gameplay.

Feel the freedom of movement and adrenaline rush — Beatrun: Revamped takes GMod parkour to the next level.
