Description
Sometimes you just need to pause and reflect.

The premise of this gamemode is to allow players to share their thoughts, whatever those may be, with others. Hear the Echoes of players across maps and servers alike, and share your thoughts with those traveling the same roads.

This was a small, one-week side-project, predominantly inspired by the 'Notes' game showcased in The Beginner's Guide (phenomenal game; play it). Funnily enough and to my surprise, many friends have drawn comparisons to Elden Ring's 'player messages', instead.

I highly recommend you disable all other addons (except for maps) before playing. This gamemode is not using Sandbox as a base, so many of your addons may error because of that.

You do not need to play on a multiplayer server for this to work. All 'Echoes' are shared with everyone who plays.

Below are a few maps that I would recommend using while playing, but any map is supported:
  • Xanthys
  • gm_mountainsummit
  • gm_grotta
  • gm_dormant
  • urban
