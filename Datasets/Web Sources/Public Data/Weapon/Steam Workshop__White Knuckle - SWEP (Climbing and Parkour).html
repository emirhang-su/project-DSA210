Description
Climb like your life depends on it!

The parkour / climbing from the game White Knuckle, faithfully recreated in Garry's Mod. Climb to your heart's content.

Just don't lose your grip, there’s eternity below you.

Instructions:
Server and clientsided options are available within the Options tab in the spawnmenu when you hold Q.

This addon currently includes four weapons:
White Knuckle: Limited stamina, only climb ledges.
Iron Knuckle: Unlimited stamina, only climb ledges.
Grub Knuckle: Limited stamina, climb any wall.
Sandbox Knuckle: Unlimited stamina, climb any wall.

Please support the original developers of the game.
https://store.steampowered.com/app/3195790/White_Knuckle/
