Description
what's this some kind of nightmare knight?!?? (wait... say that again...)

This is a swep you can find in the DELTARUNE category under weapons

find the convars at
Options --> DELTARUNE --> Roaring Knight Abilities

Controls

[LMB] - Swing Blade
[LMB + ALT] - Rapid Slashes (25 TP)
[RMB (hold)] - Crystals / Homing Swords (50+ TP)

[E] - Sword Dance (Look at target)
[E + ALT] - Fingerpoint Star Cannon -- or whatever it's called -- will buff projectiles when over 50 TP

[R] - Knife Fury (10 TP)
[R + ALT] - Starstorm/"Hyper Goner" -- or whatever it's called -- (100 TP) -- effects may be harmful to people with epilepsy, so this is your warning!

Movement Controls

[SHIFT] - Dash + Graze

[SPACEx2] - Double Jump
[SPACEx3] - Flight
[SPACE + (in flight)] - Balance

[CTRL] - Cancel Flight

Gain TP by dashing (grazing) through projectiles or by landing LMB hits
Heal by landing LMB hits

Details

- don't expect this to be absolutely perfect, as this mod can be quite laggy, and I'm just a bad programmer
- I do intend to reduce the lag in future updates
- Certain effects, *cough* starstorm *cough* can cause lag so beware!
- expect for me to make other mods that use this same combat system in the future


Credits

- deltarune by Toby Fox (play it if you somehow haven't)

- Most of the sounds were either ripped straight from ch3's data.win file or ripped from Undertale, though I did have to make a few.
- Everything else, like viewmodels, models, sprites, and particles were made by me tho C:

- playermodel from screenshots not included, though, here's the link:
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3505472585

- the map in the screenshots is called gm_erased C:

Can't use moves?

- if you can't use keys like E, R, ALT, etc. then go into settings and bind your keys to the following:
E - use, R - reload, ALT - walk, SHIFT - sprint, CTRL - crouch/duck, (these should be like this by default)

- you do NOT need to unbind them, just rebind them to the above if you're having problems

- also people are reporting that pressing alt + r is opening their gpu interface, make sure you unbind that there too if you cant use it lol
