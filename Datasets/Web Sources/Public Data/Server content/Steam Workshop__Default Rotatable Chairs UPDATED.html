Description
Almost year ago, just for fun, I took props of chairs, made them ragdolls & published it to the workshop. After seeing very positive feedback I immediately started working on update that would just add more chairs and then I completely forget about the addon. During that time, I also contacted Unknown Developer to ask him to make these props into vehicles, which players can sit and rotate. Who would have thought it would take almost an year?

There was plenty fights with physics engine to make this work efficiently, but, eventually, it ended up very well and i glad to present to you a...
Default Rotatable Chairs UPDATED

Features
  • Now, you can actually sit on them!
  • They are rotating and fully physically, even if you are sitting on them
  • If base have wheels, you can ride on these chairs

Editing Vehicle
If you In the C menu, right-click (RMB) on the base of a chair to open the customization menu. There, you can edit the seat, the base, enable or disable wheels and change viewlimit.(setting viewlimit to 0 will give you abillity to rotate camera on 360)

Controls
There's nothing difficult about handling the chairs. While you are sitting, you could press A or D to rotate a bit. If your chair has a flag wheels set, you can move forwards and backwards by pressing W or S

Other stuff
I also made a bunch of just regular props without any functions, they are just rotatable, there also separated seats and bases as props, these are required for the vehicle to work, but you are free to use them for other purposes if you wish.
