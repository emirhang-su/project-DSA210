Description
Citizen Clothing Overhaul

This addon adds a total of 55 new clothing textures that replace the ones of citizens and refugees. They are all chosen randomly for more unique looking NPCs, the clothes don't change or bug at death. There's also an options menu for modifying the citizen clothing and uniforms (admin-only on servers).

This addon is compatible with any facemap or reskin addon as long as the clothing submaterial isn't modified.
