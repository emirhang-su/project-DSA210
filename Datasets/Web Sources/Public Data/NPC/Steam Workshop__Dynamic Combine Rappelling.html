Description
If you want this to support custom animation, use this instead (Thanks UNIT1 for the compatibility fix!)

*******************************

Combines have been given implants to fight acrophobia, they will now start rappeling from high places.

Half life 2's default rappelling system of combine are scripted to be executed at a fixed distance, I wanted to make it more dynamic and work in gmod.

Features
- Combine Soldiers will now have a chance to rappel down if their target is on the lower ground
- This will prevent more situations where if they are spawned somewhere on a small high platform, they can't move at all.
- Enable their acrophobia again by typing "wblrappel_enable 0"
- wblrappel_maxdistance & wblrappel_mindistance to adjust their rappelling distance
- Combine Soldiers are now more Tacti-Cool B)

Try fighting The Combine faction now and see how it goes

Check out my similar mod for HL2 Hunters


I have a Combine AI improvement mod, or the likes. Will it conflict?
- There is a HIGH CHANCE that it will conflict, since that AI changing mod will most likely override the rappelling orders/commands. This is like having two commanders commanding the npcs (They'll keep receiving instructions on both mods and will be confused on which to execute) if both are installed. But if my mod somehow works with it, then that's good! ^_^
