Description
A huge collection of Half-Life SNPCs taken primarily from Half-Life: Extended and a few Gamebanana mods, made into their own SNPCs to help add more job diversity, and even improve the Black Mesa Workforce!

This addon is NOT in collaboration with the original mod, Half-Life: Extended, or any other mods. This is a personal pack of mine I think a lot of people would enjoy.

HALF-LIFE: RESURGENCE IS REQUIRED FOR INORDER FOR THIS PACK TO WORK!

Here's a quick, non-detailed list of some of the SNPCs added!

-NEW JOB TYPES-
New members of the Black Mesa Personnel, so the world isn't only just Scientists and Security guards!

Janitors, Service Personnel, and Construction Workers! All using custom voices from Half-Life Extended!

Office Workers, Chefs, and Survey Team Members! With brand NEW voicelines recorded and made SPECIFICALLY for this addon!

Survey Team Members are geared with an HEV suit, giving them extreme bullet resistance and higher health. Along with being armed with Pistols, Shotguns, and even a crowbar, these dudes are prepared to take down the greatest of threats.

-SECURITY TEAM-
Security has been revamped, having actual ranks resulting in different weapons and health! From starting lightweight Cadets, to Experienced Chiefs with revolvers, and even Heavy Gunners with SMGs and Shotguns, the Security force is no longer messing around! With new facial variety and cosmetics to go along with them!

Off Duty, Armorless Security guards as seen in blue-shift have been added as well.

-FEMALE STAFF TEAM-
Added Female Scientists and Female Security guards! As expected, Female Scientists and Security have their own voices as well! Female Security can spawn with different weaponry and outfits!

-SCIENCE TEAM-
Added Science instructors and Wheelchair Scientists with variety! To serve as lead members of the Science department!

Off Duty, Coatless Scientists, as seen in Blue-Shift have also been added!

As a bonus: a Low Definition version of Dr. Keller from Decay: Solo Mission is included. For those who want a Dr.Keller that fits the rest of the resurgence cast!

-SURVIVORS-
Members of the Science team who have been struggling, yet surviving throughout the disaster! Some have different abilities, and a few even wield weapons to defend themselves... Even if they aren't too great at it!

These survivors aren't randomized, and can be hand-picked due to their differing abilities.

More specific details on each SNPC will be included in a discussion page.

Another addon that's been in on and off development for awhile. This addon will most likely receive various updates content updates at random periods, this is kind of a big collection addon of cool, fun, or stupid Half-Life SNPC ideas for me.

"What about HECU? Or Xenians?"
Any other NPC added to this pack will be sticking to the theme of being members of the Black Mesa Workforce. As for Zombies, Aliens and Marines, they will be their own separate pack. But don't hold your breath, I don't know when I'm going to work on those.

Comments requesting HECU or Xenians will be deleted
