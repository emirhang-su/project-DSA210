Description
Allows (mostly) any Simfphys or grounded Glide vehicle to pursue players.
Inspired by (and somewhat a derivative of) Mad Vehicle.

Features
  • Navmesh pathfinding
  • Automatic reversing and unstucking
  • PIT/TVI maneuvers
  • Collision avoidance
  • Foot chases via NPC spawning
  • Customization

Instructions
Spawn the "Pursuit AI Enabler" NPC near any Simfphys or Glide vehicle to make it use the AI.
To change the addon's settings, go to the spawnmenu > Options > Pursuit AI > Settings.

Compatibility
The following Simfphys packs have been tested:
  • [simfphys] GTA IV Vehicles
  • [simfphys] American Truck Simulator AI Vehicles
  • [simfphys] Left 4 Dead 2 Vehicles
This addon only works with Simfphys and Glide.
