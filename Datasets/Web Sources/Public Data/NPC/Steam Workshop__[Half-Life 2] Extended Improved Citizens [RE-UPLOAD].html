What is this addon?

I always wanted there to be several variants of clothing and equipment that standard HL2 citizens could use, so I decided to create this addon.

This addon uses a Randomizer Script that replaces standard citizens with improved citizen models with different skins and bodygroups.

This addon includes:

• RNG NPCs Bodygroups and Skins;
• Various clothes for female and male citizens:
- Shirts, Pants, Glasses, Beanies and Helmets.
