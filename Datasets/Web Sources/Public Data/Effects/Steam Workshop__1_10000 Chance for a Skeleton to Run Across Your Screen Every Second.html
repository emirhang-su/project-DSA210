Description
Useless ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ addon inspired by that one Withered Foxy jumpscare mod for Terraria you may have seen on Twitter and this one South Park meme I saw once where all the kids are all like bald and then a skeleton appears running in front of them and all their eyes turn into iPhone eyes emojis.

Commands list
  • runSkel_chance - Changes the chance for the skeleton to appear to your desired value. Default is 10000, minimum is 1000
  • runSkel_test - Simple testing command that instantly deploys the running skeleton in your screen
