Here are personal mods selected for analysis. The selection criteria is as follows:

1. The mod must not be a dependency to other mods. This could inflate the statistics and we do not want that.

2. The mod should have a star rating of at least 3 stars. Mods with no stars means there are less than 25 positive and negative ratings, a very low performance not worth comparing.

There are 17 mods for both thumbnail variants, mainly because I only have that many animated thumbnail mods in my workshop and selecting the same amount of items means we can additionally do cumulative comparisons.
